## I - V - vi - IV
### Characteristics
- Very popular western progression
### Songs
- I'm Yours - Jason Mraz
- Someone like you - Adele

## I - iii - vi - iv
### Characteristics
- Variation of the previous progression, more negative because of the iii instead of the V
- Not too high energy
- Soft hopeful vibe
### Songs
- What is love - Twice
- Psycho - Red Velvet
- Spring Day - BTS
- Secrets - OneRepublic

## IV - V - iii - vi
### Characteristics
