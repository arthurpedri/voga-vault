## Nerve Glides
Lie down, grab leg above knees, gently straighten leg up hold when stretched for 1-2s then back down and repeat
## Deep Squat
Hold deep squat, move around a bit, getting comfortable in that position
## Couch Stretch
Lunge position with knee touching the ground, back foot against a wall or couch, leaning back increases the stretch
## Overhead Rotations
Holding band or stick at more than shoulder width, rotate arms from front to back while keeping hands in the same place. Reduce width between arms when it gets easy
## Cossack Squat
Squat on one leg to the side, stretching the straight leg
